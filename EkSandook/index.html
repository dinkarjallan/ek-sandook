about our bridal sandook:-

the bride sandook, is red, the most auspicious colour there is. Hand painted with traditional motifs of Bengal, the contents of this sandook were an amalgamation and a tribute to the primary elements: air, fire, earth, water. It also aimed to tease all the senses of the receiver. Packed with nostalgia, the bride sandook is a perfect gift for any and every bride.
